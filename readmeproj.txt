How to Use
Starting the Game:

Upon launching, the game will ask whether you want to load a previous game or start a new one:
Enter y to load a saved game.
Enter n to start a new game.
Selecting Categories:

For new games, choose a word category:
c: Countries
a: Animals
f: Fruits
Choosing Game Modes:

Single Player (s): Find words alone and score points.
Multiplayer (m): Play 1v1 against another player.
Versus Computer: Compete against the computer in finding words.
Gameplay:

The grid of letters is displayed, and players must identify words from the selected category.
Input the words you find to earn points.
Saving and Loading:

At any point, players can save their progress by entering s when prompted.
To resume a saved game, simply choose to load a saved game upon launching the program.
Running a Demo
To experience a quick demo:

Ensure the text files (countries.txt, animals.txt, fruits.txt) are populated with sample words.
Launch the program and choose a category and game mode.
Play through the grid or observe the computer in action (in Versus Computer mode).
Explore the save and load functionality by saving the game and resuming it later.
Enjoy playing the Word Search Game! If you encounter any issues or have suggestions, feel free to share your feedback.
